We're going for a Smart Casual / Garden Party feel — think elegant but relaxed. We want everyone to feel comfortable and enjoy the day, so please dress in a way that reflects the occasion without being too formal. Light, summery colours and breathable fabrics are encouraged — it's June in London, after all!

The reception will take place in the Gated Garden at St Anne's Church, so please bear in mind you will be on grass for much of the afternoon.

A smart suit or blazer with trousers works perfectly — a tie is entirely optional. Light colours such as cream, navy, sage or tan are a great fit for the garden party atmosphere. Avoid very casual items like jeans or trainers.

Suggested: Linen or cotton suit, chinos with a blazer, loafers or smart shoes.

A midi or maxi dress, a smart jumpsuit, or a skirt and blouse would be ideal. Florals, pastels and earthy tones all work beautifully for a summer garden party. Please feel free to wear colour — the brighter the better!

One small note: as the reception is on grass, we'd gently suggest leaving the stilettos at home — block heels, wedges or flats will keep you much more comfortable through the afternoon!
